Instruction Sets for Strangers

Role: Experience Designer

Tools + Methods: Research, Observation

Deliverables: Process Work, Case Study

It's a school project, yes, but it is also the first time I started understanding what UX Design is, and that it's what I wanted to spend my time doing. It's the spark that set me down this career path, and a reminder of the joy I have felt in this field. In short, this project tasked us with observing 'users' at a specified location, then creating two interventions based on our gathered information, observing, then choosing one to iterate on further. It was lovely.
