Project Overview
This is a short fun fact booklet all about french fries! I wanted to do a fun project where I could explore typography and visual layouts and I thought that french fries would be a fun topic to do that with. I designed the layout, planned the spreads, made the visuals, and put it together.

The booklet is folded accordion style and has 12 pages, or 6 spreads which vary in layout and text formatting. Around the book itself is a bellyband which looks like a box of fries. The backs of all of the pages have a 'spilled fries' pattern which works with the bellyband to create the illusion of fries coming from the box.
