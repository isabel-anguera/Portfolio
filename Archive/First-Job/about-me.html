About Me

Hi there! My name is Isabel Anguera. I am a recent graduate of Parsons School of Design, having majored in Design and Technology (BFA), and Culture and Media Studies (BA). I love working withiin all the areas of digital product design, and merging design with social change/commentary. I am based in New York, though I have lived in a few different places throughout my life, including Peru, Portugal, and a couple of different East coast states in the US. I love meeting new people and learning from them and I can't wait to be a part of a super cool team!
