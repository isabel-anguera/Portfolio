French Fry Fun Facts

Role: Visual Designer

Tools + Methods: Adobe InDesign, Photoshop,
Illustrator

Deliverables: Digital and Physical Booklet

French Fry Fun Facts is a fun study in typography and layouts, where I created an accordion-style booklet filled with facts and stories about French fries. The booklet contains four two-page spreads and flips through like a book, though it can be extended to be read as one long strip.

The back pages of the booklet are laid out with a French fry pattern, as if they were spread along a table to be shared. When folded up completely, the booklet fits into a book sleeve, made to look like a fast-food French fry holder, and is cut halfway down to play with the back pages of the booklet itself, which makes it look as if the fries are spilling out of the sleeve.
